⚽ World Cup 2026 Bracket Challenge 🏆

48 teams · 12 groups · pick your qualifiers, fill the knockout bracket, beat your friends.

1 · Group Stage

In each group, click teams in your predicted finishing order: 1st, 2nd, 3rd (4th fills automatically). Top two advance. Click a ranked team to re-pick from there.

2 · Best Third-Place Teams

Eight of the twelve third-place teams advance to the Round of 32. Pick which eight make it.

3 · Knockout Bracket

The Round of 32 fills in automatically from your group picks, paired exactly how FIFA lays out the bracket. Click winners to advance them.

As the real tournament unfolds, enter the actual group standings, the real best-thirds, and knockout winners here. Everyone's score updates against these. One friend can maintain results and share the code — paste it on the Leaderboard tab to sync.
